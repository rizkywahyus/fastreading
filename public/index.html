About FastReading

FastReading is a free, no-login RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) speed reading tool. RSVP presents text word-by-word at a fixed focal point, eliminating the saccadic eye movements that slow traditional reading by up to 30%.

Whether you're clearing a reading backlog, studying for exams, or just trying to consume more: FastReading helps you read smarter, not just faster.
